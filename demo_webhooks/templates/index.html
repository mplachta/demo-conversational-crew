CrewAI Conversational Agents Demo

This demo showcases the power of CrewAI's conversational agents. Experience AI-driven conversations that adapt to your inputs and provide intelligent responses.

The Crew answers questions related to Chase Freedom Benefits.

The crew is processing the messages in the following way:

  • First it classifies the message into one of the categories: pleasentries, chase card benefits related questions, other questions
  • Pleasentries are processed by a single, lite agent
  • Chase card benefits related questions entries are answered by a Crew that is using a knowledge from a PDF file
  • Other questions do not call an agent and just return a generic response asking for a different question
